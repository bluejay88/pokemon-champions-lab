Available Pokémon

In Pokémon Champions, as it is solely a battle focused game, not all Pokémon are available within it. Instead, there are a set number of Pokémon available. While you are able to read your Pokémon from Pokémon HOME into the game, you can also purchase rental of species using Victory Points after getting them in recruit ranch

Some Pokémon are also given to players as gifts.
